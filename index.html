Gluten Free Peanut Butter Cookies

Crispy and bursting with peanut butter flavor, these cookies are so good you wont believe they're really gluten free!

Ingredients

  • 8 TBSP Butter, softened
  • 3 Cups Creamy Peanut Butter
  • 1.5 Cups Brown Sugar
  • 1 Tsp Vanilla
  • 4 Eggs
  • 3 Cups Flour
  • 2 Tsp Baking Soda
  • 1/2 Tsp Salt

Instructions

  1. Combine Butter, Peanut Butter, Brown Sugar, Vanilla until creamy.
  2. Add Eggs one at a time, mixing after each addition.
  3. When fully combined, gradually add Flour, Baking Soda, and Salt.
  4. Bake at 350 degrees for 15 minutes.
